These maps are generated by amalgamating election results from polling places and where/how in a particular area voted, thus by treating ea voter as casting the average amount of each type of vote we can estimate what % of people voted for a party in a small area (both data sets are from the Australian Electoral Commission)


The maps are searchable, and information about each area will display when hovered over. Furthermore, as colours get darker it represents a greater amount of votes for that party (Note: these scales aren't uniform as the maximum One Nation vote, for instance, is smaller than the largest Labor vote share)


Each area represents a Statistical Area 1 (SA1) which represent somewhere between 100-1000 people (not voters!), the larger outlined areas represent electoral divisions.
